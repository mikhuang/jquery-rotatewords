Rotate Words

A fun, dumb, silly, cool, fad-ish plugin for jQuery by Michael Huang (@mikhuang)

Wrap a list of words; sayings; things; this, that, the other and watch them go! They can go fast, speedy, zippy, lightning. Or perhaps slow|tardy|not, very, fast|meh. A,S,D,FK,L,P,QR,F,H,KL,M,N,B

$('.rotatewords').rotatewords();
$('.rotatewords2').rotatewords({
  'delim': ';',
  'interval': 3000
});
$('.rw').rotatewords({
  'interval': 200
});
